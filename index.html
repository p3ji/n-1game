How to Play N-1 Word Craft

  1. Spell words (3+ letters) using the cardboard wheel's letters.
  2. Typed/clicked letters appear in the center of the wheel.
  3. Find all possible words except one to unlock the next level word package.
  4. When you submit a correct word, Boxy will eat it and one of the mini boxes above will pop open!
  5. Buy the next starter word package (which shrinks to 6, then 5, then 4 letters).
  6. Complete the 4-letter level to claim ultimate Victory!

💡 Tip: Click the center of the wheel to submit. Shuffling is free!
