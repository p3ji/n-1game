How to Play N-1 Word Craft

  1. You start with a 7-letter word at Level 1.
  2. Spell other words (3+ letters) using only the letters of this word.
  3. Each letter can only be used as many times as it appears in the starter word.
  4. Find all possible words except one to unlock the next level package in the shop.
  5. Purchase the next starter word (which will be 6 letters).
  6. Continue this loop from 6 letters → 5 letters → 4 letters.
  7. Complete the 4-letter word level to claim ultimate Victory!

💡 Tip: Earn points to buy hints. Shuffling letters is free!
